Renegade Reformer is a community-focused movement studio offering Reformer Pilates, Mat Pilates, Yoga and Barre. We are looking for instructors who take real pride in their craft and want to be part of a studio that values both teaching and teaching environment.

Bristol, UK  ·  Freelance with growth potential  ·  Now recruiting

The Studio
Who we are.

Our focus is on delivering exceptionally taught classes rooted in strong technique, innovative programming and a high standard of client experience. Our mission is to provide an inclusive and empowering fitness environment that caters to individuals of all fitness levels.

We have created a spacious, well-equipped studio designed to support Pilates sessions that feel intimate and personal. Our studio environment, sound and lighting have been carefully considered to support focus, energy and flow within each class - creating an experience that feels distinctive and immersive.

The Person
Who you are.
  • Take real pride in your craft and hold yourself to a high standard
  • Warm, professional and confident in the room
  • Bring energy, control and a strong sense of presence to each class
  • Care deeply about teaching correct technique, not just delivering a routine
  • Have a strong knowledge of anatomy relevant to Pilates and injury prevention
  • Understand the power of music and atmosphere to motivate movement
  • Maintain exceptional standards of cleanliness and care within the studio
The Role
What you'll do.
  • Deliver high-quality Reformer Pilates classes with a strong technical foundation
  • Confidently coach clients of mixed abilities with clear regressions and progressions
  • Lead the room with energy, precision and control
  • Contribute to a consistent class experience aligned with the Renegade standard
  • Represent the studio at community events and brand collaborations where required
Requirements
What we need.
  • A minimum of 1 year's teaching experience post-qualification
  • A recognised Reformer Pilates qualification - STOTT, BASI or APPI preferred, including Level 3 Mat Pilates
  • A strong understanding of movement mechanics and class programming
We will only consider applicants with a full Reformer Pilates qualification as a minimum. If you don't meet every criteria, use your cover letter to outline relevant background - dance, ballet, gymnastics, athletic performance, personal training or other movement disciplines are all welcomed.
