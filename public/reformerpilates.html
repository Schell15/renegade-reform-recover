Our approach

Reformer Pilates in Bristol —
a divinely powerful approach,
fusing strength and control with rhythm.

Movement at Renegade is motivated by music, where tradition is met with progression. We elevate pilates as a source of personal power — one that makes you feel bold and speaks to the inner desire to use movement as a release.

Renegade is an immersive pilates experience. We introduce a new concept to Bristol, fusing pilates, fitness, set design and world-class audio and lighting production to guide your flow — designed and built by some of Bristol's finest creative minds. The first of its kind in the UK.
