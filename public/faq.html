Cancellations & no-shows

We operate a 24-hour cancellation policy for every class: drop-in, class pack or membership.

Cancel more than 24 hours before your class and your credit or booking is returned to your account automatically. Cancel within 24 hours and it's a late cancellation, the credit is lost. Cancel within 90 minutes of the class and a £5 late cancellation fee also applies.

To cancel, log in to your account on Momence and select "cancel yourself" on the booking.

A £10 no-show fee applies. Always check in on the front desk iPad when you arrive to avoid this being applied by mistake.

It keeps classes running properly and lets us offer your spot to someone on the waitlist. As a small studio, we can't run with sudden half-empty classes. Even cancelling within the 24-hour window still helps another member take your place. We appreciate your cooperation.

Memberships

Every reformer class costs 3 credits. Depending on your plan, you get 12, 24 or 36 credits a month (equivalent to 4, 8 or 12 reformer classes). Credits renew on your billing date, don't roll over if unused, and don't cover Yoga, Barre or Mat Pilates.

Membership fees are paid monthly in advance by card, direct debit or another approved method, and renew automatically each month on your billing date until you cancel. If a payment fails, access to bookings and remaining credits may be temporarily suspended until it's resolved.

Cancel through your Momence account or by emailing studio@renegadereformer.co.uk, giving at least 14 days' notice before your next billing date. Verbal requests, Instagram DMs or messages to instructors don't count as formal notice. If we get less than 14 days' notice, your next payment may still be taken and the membership will end the following billing cycle.

Membership freezes are not offered as standard. In exceptional circumstances, such as illness, injury or extended travel, Renegade Reformer may agree to pause a membership at its discretion.

Requests must be made at least 7 days in advance by emailing studio@renegadereformer.co.uk and may require supporting information.

Your data

We collect what we need to manage your bookings, membership, payments, studio access and safety, including contact details, emergency contact, health information and waiver responses.
